------------------Features------------------

* Players can choose Rock, Paper or Scissors and has to compete against a Bot.
* Also has a feature to show scores
* Players can enter their name, which will be displayed throughout the game.
* Players can exit the game at any time by pressing Q or q.
* Follows the classic rules where:
    i. Rock beats Scissors
    ii. Scissors beats Paper
    iii. Paper beats Rock
* The Bot randomly selects Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
